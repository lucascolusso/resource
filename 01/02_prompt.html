Design exercise prompt. Read carefully! :-)

Imagine your are a designer at Facebook. We discovered that Facebook is used by a massive amount of people to ask for help. For example, a teenager struggling with her math homework, a friend asking for advice with a relationship issue, or a foreigner trying to understand the American Democratic x Republican debate.

You have to create a feature, or modify a feature, to maximize the number of answers to posts where people are searching for some type of help.

You will have one week to complete the exercise. You can take on each proposed step (as seen in the list below) in separate days or in a single-shot, as you wish! The following schedule is a suggestion; you can fit it to your needs, constraints or preferred design process:

  1. Ideation ~ 30 minutes. Generate ideas and sketches for the feature.
  2. Design ~ 1 hour. Select the best idea you generated in the first stage and polish it.
