We suggest you spending 1 hour in ideation, 1 hour in design and 30 minutes preparing Guide our friend Flappy Bird through the pipes without crashing into them or letting the Flappy Bird fall on the ground.

You're joining a gaming group. Members' performances will be compared.

1. In the next screen, press "Spacebar" on your keyboard to start the first round.

"Spacebar" is also the key you need to press to make Flappy Bird fly higher (i.e., flap wings).

2. After each round, you will see a popup with information about your performance.

In the popup, you will also have the option to play a new round or exit the game and continue to the final survey.

To play a new round, click the "Play again" button.

To exit the game and continue to the final survey, click the "Exit game" button.
