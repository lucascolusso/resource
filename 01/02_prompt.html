Design exercise prompt. Read carefully! :-)

We challenge you to create a new feature for Facebook. People might want to post a question, or ask for help in various situations — maybe they want a friend's help to take care of their pets when they are about to travel, or for more serious affairs, for example they discovered someone in their family has a disease, or their car was robbed. The feature you will create intends on maximizing the number of comments or messages connected to posts like this — when someone asks for help.

You will have one week to execute the 3 stages below. You can take on them in separate days or in a single-shot, as you wish! We suggest the following schedule, which can and should be adapted to your needs, constraints or design process:

1. Ideation » 1 hour. Generate ideas and sketches for the feature design. These can be both short sentences or drawings. The ideas and sketches can be rough, low-fidelity, however they have to make sense to you, as in the final stage we will ask you to annotate them.
Take note of how many minutes you spend in this stage.
2. Design » 1 hour. Select one of the ideas from the first stage. You will develop the idea as much as you can, aiming at reaching what you consider to be a finalized design. With this design, the intention is having something that can be shown to other designers and communicate your idea to solve the design challenge.
Take note of how many minutes you spend in this stage.
3. Submit » 30 minutes. Prepare the outcomes of your work to submit us and answer a short survey about the exercise. There are examples of what materials we expect you to submit in the next page, but now focus on the exercise! :-)

Important!

1. Support. You’ll use this resource as a reference for your work: Design card. Click on the link to open the file. You can also use any other website or forum you currently use to inform your design process.
