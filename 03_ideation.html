1. Ideation

Generate ideas and sketches for the feature. These can be both short sentences or drawings. The ideas and sketches can be rough, low-fidelity, however, they have to make sense to you, as we will ask you to annotate them.

Challenge. Your job is to create a feature or modify a Facebook feature to maximize the number of answers for posts where people are searching for some kind of help.

Suggested time for this activity: 30 minutes

Upload the following two materials to this folder

1. PDF with scans or photographs

Send us your sketches/paragraphs if you used physical mediums to produce them. Annotate your ideas, explaining the concepts so that others can read and understand.

2. Video - Screen recording of the exercise

Record your computer screen during Ideation and Design. In stage 1, Ideation, you will probably sketch by hand. That's ok, we are interested in the web searches you do to inform or inspire your design. Later you will also submit scans or pictures of your sketches and we will analyse them too.

Follow this Quicktime recording tutorial. If you are a Mac user, you already have Quicktime installed. If you are a Windows user, download and install Quicktime or use another screen recording app of your choice. Think out loud if you feel comfortable so we can better understand your thought process.
